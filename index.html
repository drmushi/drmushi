Biography

Ms. Doreen Richard Mushi is an educational technologist with over seven years extensive experience in E-learning, Online Facilitation and Open Educational Resources (OER). She is currently pursuing a doctoral program in Teacher Education and Learning Sciences at North Carolina State University in USA. Her Ph.D. research is focused at investigating the sustainability of learnng analytics projects in higher learning institutions.
Prior to joining NCSTATE, Ms. Mushi had worked as an educational technologist at the Open University of Tanzania (OUT) under its Institute of Educational Management and Technologies (IEMT). Her main role has been to support the administration and integration of Information and Communication Technologies in teaching and learning processes at the university.
You can read more about Ms. Mushi's Ph.D. journey at NCSTATE here.
