Projects

Open Educational Resources(OER) Facilitation

I am one of the co-developers and facilitators of Working with Open Educational Resources module at the Open University of Tanzania.
The module is a component of Digital Fluency course for academics which has been developed in collaboration with OER Africa.

TusomeInnovations

TusomeInnovations is a startup that provides access to digital learning resources to children with hearing disability in Tanzania.
Through the startup I have been able to lead the digitization of sign language dictionary that was initially developed by Tanzatoto Foundation.
